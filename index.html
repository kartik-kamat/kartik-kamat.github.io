Research experience

Computational and statistical mechanical approaches for problems in thermodynamics and kinetics.

1) My graduate research work is on a method development project for computing free-energy differences between different crystal structures.

2) We are also formulating an analytically tractable kinetic model for understanding the attachment of antifreeze proteins. We use custom code simulations to validate our theoretical predictions.(in collaboration with Prof. Valeria Molinero's group)
